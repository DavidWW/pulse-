Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Summary

Pulsos+ is a health reports and widgets app for iOS. Your privacy is our top priority. We do not collect, store, or share your personal or health data. All information stays on your device.

Health Data (HealthKit)

Pulsos+ accesses Apple HealthKit data solely to display your health metrics within the app and generate PDF reports. This data:

  • Is read directly from HealthKit on your device.
  • Is never sent to external servers.
  • Is never stored outside your device.
  • Is never shared with third parties.
  • Is never used for advertising or tracking purposes.

PDF reports you generate are created locally on your device. You decide whether to share them.

Categories of Data Accessed

The app may request read access to the following HealthKit categories, depending on your configuration:

  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Blood pressure
  • Blood oxygen
  • Body temperature
  • Weight, BMI, body fat, and lean body mass
  • Sleep data
  • Stand hours
  • Active and resting calories
  • Menstrual cycle
  • Workout activities

Data We Do NOT Collect

Pulsos+ does not collect any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Name, email, or contact information.
  • Location or geolocation data.
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes.
  • Usage data for analytics or advertising.

Analytics

Pulsos+ may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as number of reports generated or widgets configured) to improve the app. This data cannot personally identify you and never includes health data.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Apple. We do not have access to or store your payment information.

Security

Your health data is protected by iOS and HealthKit security measures, including device encryption. Pulsos+ does not implement additional data transmission mechanisms because there is no transmission: everything stays on your device.

Children

Pulsos+ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with the corresponding update date.
